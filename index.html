About Us


What is BRIDGE?
We are a social enterprise that strives to bridge the gap between NGOs and volunteers.
Our aim is to connect you with the NGOs that need your skills the most, enabling you to make a difference that matters.

Our Mission


To join the helping hand of passionate student volunteers and the NGOs that want to create a social media presence and reach out to the youth, providing the manpower they need for their day-to-day activities — and to help you enact the change you wish to see in our world.
